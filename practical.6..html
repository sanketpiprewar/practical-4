Shah Rukh Khan

Biography

Shah Rukh Khan, also known as SRK, is an Indian actor, film producer, and television personality, born on November 2, 1965, in New Delhi. Often referred to as the "King of Bollywood", he has appeared in over 80 Bollywood films and earned numerous accolades including 14 Filmfare Awards.

He began his career in television in the late 1980s and rose to fame with his debut film "Deewana" in 1992. Known for his romantic roles, charismatic presence, and emotional performances, SRK quickly became one of the most loved and highest-paid actors in the world.

Legacy

SRK is more than just an actor. He is an icon of success, hard work, and humility. From his humble beginnings to being the co-owner of Red Chillies Entertainment and the Kolkata Knight Riders IPL team, his entrepreneurial spirit matches his artistic excellence.

He is also a global figure, featured on international platforms like TED Talks and Time magazine's 100 most influential people list. SRK supports numerous charities and education initiatives, making him a true superstar both on and off screen.
